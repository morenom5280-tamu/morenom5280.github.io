Media Usage Mind Map

Challenge/Difficxulty

One challenge I experienced was with TikTok and its algorithm. I found the way it constantly served me political content to be upsetting, and I realized I was spending more time scrolling than I wanted. The short-form nature of the content felt addictive and like it was “short-circuiting” my thinking, making it harder to focus or engage deeply with ideas. To address this, I decided to delete TikTok from my phone. Since then, I’ve been a little more disconnected from some trending content, but important topics still come up naturally in conversations. This experience highlighted how algorithms and short-form media can be mentally draining, even when the content seems harmless or entertaining.
